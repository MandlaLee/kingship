About Us

Building Kingdom leaders through worship, discipleship, purpose, and revival.

Our Story

Kingship Christian Centre is a Christ-centered ministry passionate about transforming lives, restoring hope, and empowering people to walk boldly in purpose.

We believe church should be more than a Sunday gathering. It should be a family, a movement, and a place where people genuinely encounter God.

Through worship, mentorship, outreach, youth empowerment, and discipleship, we are committed to raising a generation rooted in faith, leadership, and excellence.

Our Vision

To raise Kingdom-minded leaders who influence society through Christ, purpose, integrity, and revival.

Our Mission

To create environments where people encounter God, grow spiritually, discover purpose, and impact the world.

Our Culture

We value love, authenticity, worship, community, excellence, leadership, and spiritual growth.

Leadership
